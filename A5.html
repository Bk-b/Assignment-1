My Top 5 Movies

  1. Avenger:Endgame
    • Release Date: 26 April 2019
    • Cast:Robert Downey Jr., Chris Hemsworth, Chris Evans
    • Description: "Avengers: Endgame" is a 2019 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team 'the Avengers'.
  2. Spider-Man: Into The Spider-Verse
    • Release Date: 14 December 2018
    • Cast: Shameik Moore, Jake Johnson, Hailee Steinfeld
    • Description: "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" is a 2018 animated superhero film featuring the character Miles Morales as Spider-Man.
  3. Jujutsu Kaisen: Chapter 0
    • Release Date:
    • Cast: Kayleigh McKee, Yuichi Nakamura, Takahiro Sakurai
    • Description: "Jujutsu Kaisen 0" is a prequel film to the popular anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen." It follows Yuta Okkotsu, a high school student who becomes a sorcerer to control the cursed spirit of his childhood friend, Rika Orimoto.
  4. Rush Hour 3
    • Release Date: 10 August 2007
    • Cast:Jackie Chan, Chris Tucker, Hiroyuki Sanada
    • Description: "Rush Hour 3" follows Inspector Lee (played by Jackie Chan) and Detective Carter (played by Chris Tucker) as they head to Paris to protect a French woman who possesses vital information about the secret leaders of the Chinese Triads.
  5. Intersteller
    • Release Date: 15 November 2014
    • Cast: Matthew McConaughe, Jessica Chastain, Anne Hathaway
    • Description: "Interstellar" is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, where humanity faces extinction due to environmental collapse. The story follows a farmer and former NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, who leads a team of astronauts traveling through a wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable planet for humanity.
